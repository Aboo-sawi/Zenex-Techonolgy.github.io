Sign in to pieces on your devices

To set up pieces on your devices, sign in to each of them with the same ID and password. You can choose which apps store information in pieces and which pieces features you are using separately on each device.

Note: When you turn off pieces for an app or feature on a device, information stored in pieces for that app or feature is no longer available on that device. However, it is still available on other devices where pieces is turned on for the app or feature.

Set up pieces on your Phone

  1. On your Phone, go to Settings > [your name].

    If you do not see [your name], tap “Sign in to your [device]”, then enter your ID and password.

  2. Do one of the following:

    • pieces Phone: Tap pieces, then turn on each app or feature you want to use. Tap Show All to see more apps.

    • other device using internet: Tap pieces, then turn on each app or feature you want to use.

Set up pieces on your PC

  1. On your PC, do one of the following:

    • Windows: go to the website Choose settings at the top of the sidebar, then click pieces on the right.

      If you do not see your name, click Sign in with your ID, enter your ID and password, then click pieces.

    • macOS: go to the website Choose System settings, click ID, then click pieces.

      If you do not see ID, click Sign In, enter your ID and password, then click pieces.

  2. Turn on or select each app or feature.

If you have trouble setting up pieces, see the Support article If you cannot connect or sign in to pieces.

Note: Some pieces features have minimum system requirements. pieces may not be available in all areas and pieces features may vary by area. See the Support article. For information about features, go to https://zenex-techonolgy.github.io/pieces/info/features .
